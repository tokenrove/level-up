What is this site, and why does it exist?

The fundamental idea behind Level Up! was to make life more like videogames, and to try and harness some of the simple but powerful feedback that classic role-playing games use to keep a player going.

Can't I just add arbitrary XP? Is that cheating?
The only person you can cheat is yourself, since the purpose of Level Up! is self-motivation and productivity. There is the Hall of Heroes, but it's mostly for fun. Players who appear to be abusing things just to get on the Hall will simply not appear on the Hall, at the discretion of the administrators.
